# 005 - Used-Car Price Estimator

## Goal

Estimate a fair price range for used cars from confirmed structured attributes.

## Planned Scope

- Train and evaluate only on appropriate used-car records.
- Do not blindly train one model on mixed new and used inventory.
- Use new-car listings for search, comparison, warranty alternatives, and
  reference pricing, not as ordinary training rows for the used-car estimator.
- Report MAE/RMSE and limitations; never guarantee vehicle value.
