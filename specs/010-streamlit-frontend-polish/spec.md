# 010 - Streamlit Frontend Polish

## Goal

Provide a clear, demo-ready Streamlit interface for the stable backend
capabilities without duplicating backend business logic.

## Planned Scope

- Chat with session continuity and visible source references.
- Inventory search and car detail display.
- Recommendation and 2-to-5 car comparison views.
- Used-car price-check form with disclaimer and validation feedback.
- Dealer inquiry confirmation flow.
- Future guarded image upload and analysis view.
- Clear loading, empty, error, and safe-refusal states.
