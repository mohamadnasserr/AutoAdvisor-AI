# Lebanon Car Buying Notes

AutoAdvisor AI is designed for Lebanon-first car-buying guidance with MENA-ready expansion.

## Currency

Many car listings in Lebanon are priced in USD. Buyers should still confirm payment method, cash price, transfer terms, and any additional fees.

## Market risks

Used-car buyers should be careful about accident history, flood damage, mileage rollback, imported vehicles with unclear history, and missing service records.

## Inspection

A pre-purchase inspection is strongly recommended before paying a deposit. The inspection should check mechanical condition, electronics, chassis condition, paintwork, tires, brakes, and diagnostic codes.

## Documents

Buyers should verify ownership papers, registration documents, customs status when relevant, and whether the vehicle has legal or financial issues.

## Negotiation

Negotiation should consider mileage, year, condition, service history, tire condition, accident history, and market alternatives. A low price is not always a good deal if major repairs are needed.

## Dealer vs private seller

Dealers may provide more support, warranty, or trade-in options, while private sellers may offer lower prices. The buyer should compare total risk, not only listed price.
